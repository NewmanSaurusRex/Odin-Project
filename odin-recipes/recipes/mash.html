Mashed Potatoes!

Make this mash a few days ahead and store them in the fridge! (If they last that long...)

    Ingredients
  • 5 lbs Yukon Gold potatoes, cubed
  • 8oz heavy cream
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 block cheddar cheese, grated
  • 1 stick of salted butter
  • salt and pepper to taste

    • If you plan on serving these right away, preheat the oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C) before you start cooking the potatoes in Step 1. Then follow Steps 1, 2, and 5.
      Steps:
    1. Place cubed potatoes in a large part of lightly salted water. Bring to a boil and cook until potatoes are tender, usually about 15-20 mins; drain.
    2. Transfer potatoes into a large bowl and mash. Stir in heavy cream, milk, cheese, butter, salt, and pepper until well combined. Transfer into a large casserole dish.
    3. Let potatoes cool completely, 15-20 minutes. Cover with aluminum foil and refrigerate for up to 2 days. >
    4. When ready to bake, remove casserole from the refrigerator and let sit for 30 minutes on the counter. Preheat the oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C).
    5. Bake, covered, in the preheated oven for 50 minutes.
